2 personas have been created to show how users will navigate and use the application...

Adding new Item


Henry Jones

  • 78 years old
  • Lives alone
  • Has light dementia

Henry wants to remember where he placed his newspaper, so he opens the app presses the floating button in the bottom left corner with the ‘+’ symbol on to create a new item. This will then open the ‘new item model’ he will then be prompted to enter the information for the item, such as the name, a photo, reminder date, and the location (This can be selected from a map)



Find item


Alice Madsen

  • 32 years old
  • Collector

Alice has moved some of her collection to a warehouse and wants to update this in the app so she can keep track. She opens the app and selects the ‘Items’ tab, this then displays the list of her items, she uses the search button on the toolbar at the top of the screen to find the items easier. When she originally made the items, she added the same tag to all of them, this allows her to just search the tag name to load the items she wants. She can then press and hold on one of the items to perform a multiselect to modify a group of items at once, she then presses edit and changes the location to the warehouse using the map. She follows the same steps and modifies other items that she has not yet moved to the warehouse to send her a reminder when she can move more items there.
