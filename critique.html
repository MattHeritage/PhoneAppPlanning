To help get a better understanding of what the app should include, 2 compariosions have been made with other simular apps...


The first app to look at is 'Sortly' by 'Sortly Inc.' It's an inventory system aimed at businesses to help track of the stock they have to sell.


Press to enlarge

This image of Sortly shows the create item screen. The design of the page looks very nice with everything clearly laid out this app doesn't allow users to save a location or set reminders for items.


Press to enlarge

This image displays the Sortly items page. Sortly can list items in a grid view (Shown in image) and in a list view. Having this option is good because it means its easier to find items.

While both of these applications seem excellent, they are both aimed at use for companies to maintain their sock values. 'Save it: Item Finder' will be primarily developed for the casual user who just needs to keep track of their possessions.
